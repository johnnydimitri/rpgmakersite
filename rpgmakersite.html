Popular RPG Maker Games

  • Mad Father: Mad Father is a chilling horror game that delves into the dark secrets of a young girl, Aya Drevis. Living in an eerie mansion with her father, a mad scientist, Aya uncovers horrifying experiments and must navigate a night filled with supernatural occurrences. The game is rich in storytelling, with a haunting atmosphere, puzzles, and multiple endings based on player choices.
  • The Coffin of Andy and Lele: The Painful: The Coffin of Andy and Leyley is a unique and surreal RPG that blends dark humor with poignant storytelling. Set in a bleak, post-apocalyptic world, the game follows siblings Andy and Leyley as they navigate grim challenges, uncover haunting truths, and fight to survive. Its quirky art style, emotional depth, and engaging combat mechanics make it a standout RPG Maker title.
  • LiEat: LiEat is a whimsical and heartwarming trilogy of short RPGs about Efina, a dragon who consumes lies, and her mysterious companion, Leo. Each chapter revolves around uncovering secrets, solving puzzles, and battling creatures born from lies. With charming pixel art, an engaging story, and light RPG mechanics, LiEat is a delightful blend of fantasy and mystery.
  • Ib: Ib is a surreal horror game that places the player in the shoes of a young girl trapped in a haunted art gallery. The gallery is filled with unsettling paintings and sculptures that come to life, creating a tense and eerie atmosphere. Players solve puzzles, interact with strange characters, and uncover the galleryâ€™s dark secrets in an unforgettable narrative-driven experience.

Why Make Your Own RPG?

Creating an RPG allows you to tell your story, share adventures, and engage others in a world of your own creation. RPG Maker makes it possible for anyone to design a game with rich narratives and captivating characters.
